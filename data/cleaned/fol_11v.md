## Notes
Four pairs of wrestlers in unarmed combat. Top-left figure wears a crown (master). Bottom-left pair shows a close grappling exchange with one figure wearing a crown (master). Bottom-right shows two figures in close engagement with one wearing a short tunic. The text is heavily abbreviated and difficult to read in several places. Many readings are uncertain.

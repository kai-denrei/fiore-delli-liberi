## Notes
- Folio 38 is misbound in the physical manuscript (MS Ludwig XV 13). It belongs between folios 14 and 15, as it contains additional dagger plays (abrazare/dagger material) rather than the sword/poleaxe content of the surrounding folios 37 and 39.
- Four pairs of figures shown, all depicting unarmed/dagger grappling combat (abrazare)
- Figures are unarmored, shown in short tunics with visible hose
- The crowned figure (master) appears in the dominant position in each pair
- The combat shown is wrestling/grappling, consistent with the dagger section of the treatise
- This misplacement is well-documented in scholarly literature on the manuscript

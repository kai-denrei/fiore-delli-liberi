## Notes
Four pairs of wrestlers. Top-left shows a crowned figure (master) applying what appears to be an arm lock or throw on another figure who is wearing a cloak/cape. Top-right shows two figures in a standing exchange. Bottom-left shows two figures in close grappling with arm bindings. Bottom-right shows two figures wrestling on the ground or in a low position. This continues the ligadure (binding/locking) section. Text throughout is very faded and uncertain.

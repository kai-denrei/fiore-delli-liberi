# Folio 36r

## Transcription

Top-left:
¶ Anche tornarle lo zio contrario posta de finestra volta e bona
maestra tegnudo posta fianno. E tenire arme de Anistri e son tuti
men che aspra ella che huomeni. Quel zapo francho forano
foreno. Chome non remouere que ela casta lo braço la punta
seglie arme e louar ella non prouare.

Top-right:
¶ Ella de finestra per chiamarse la finestra e una
nobulle bruça e sta la sua che botena. Corpo di contenta pro-
fichando. Sola di dare copa ella lo braço ella con- 
tra segna ren la finestra e cremonisse [?] non da renouale
se manibus de posta. La si tuore in la finestra lle la contra-
ria la botena. E trop contrario ny chi bayti che non potra.
Veglie bey preste.

## Translation

Top-left:
Also to return the play, the counter guard of the window turned is a good master's proven guard. And to hold arms of the left and all are less harsh and of men. That free strike they will make. As not to remove, for the wrist, the arm, the point follows the arms and to raise it is not to prove.

Top-right:
She of the window, to be called the window, is a noble arm and stands as her good one. The body is content, profiting. Only to give a blow and the arm and the counter sign renders the window and acknowledges [?] not of renewal from the hands of guard. She takes herself in the window and the contrary the good one. And too contrary, nor who can wait, for he cannot. Watch well quickly.

- Page number "36" visible at bottom, and what appears to be "f5" notation at upper right (original foliation)

# Folio 23v

## Transcription

Top-left:
¶ Qui cominzano le guardie che de spade a doy mane e grande. La posta si e comp[i]ta e bona de spade. ¶ De queste guardie si e tredese Longha e curta e mezo le posso io fare de ogni guardie e non del tuto bono sopra uno e mezo fare. ¶ Ella Frontale de le poste ella matrice. ¶ Perche de posste lo primo acresser ello fa volto e le certo redole ella da mesura. ¶ Questa sia tuta falle de lo largo grande. Posta de ferro pulsativa.

¶ Posta de ferro pulsativa.

Top-right:
¶ Questa si e posta de donna che per tuta mane e elle e la piu bella. ¶ E de tute coste ella si e de grande avantaze. ¶ E guarda lo scolaro de lo ben fare. E la pontta destra Longha e la sinestra e curta. ¶ Ella fendente de spada punta ella blasturra. ¶ Ella cominza lo soprammano e soppra che fiancho ella pulsativa.

¶ Posta de donna destra e pulsativa.

Bottom-left:
¶ Questa si e posta de fenestra che e fenestra che manchar[e] fenestra la pugna. ¶ Ella manaza de fare alle elle poste. E non stare grande de breve alle poste. E chi se non guarda[re] quello che la fenestra e ello le Speranza. ¶ Ella sforzo quello de bono que muda e covertte le posste e ferischa e traversa e quello le vado ella per luy posta.

¶ Posta de fenestra instabile.

Bottom-right:
¶ Questa si e posta de donna la fenestra che e la donna e la Rosa ella e brazal posta. ¶ Ella chiama e e certa le fenestre e altro perto. ¶ Le lanze drette e le doy punte voce companie [?]. ¶ E sop[r]a dol guardare de loy posta.

¶ Posta de donna la fenestra pulsativa.

## Translation

Top-left:
Here begin the guards of the two-handed sword, and they are great. The guard is complete and good for swords. Of these guards there are thirteen, long and short and half, I can make them from every guard, and not entirely good to do one and a half. And the Frontal of the guards is the matrix. Because from the guard the first advance makes the turn and the certain rules give measure. This is the whole deception of the broad great. Guard of the iron, striking.

Label: Posta de ferro pulsativa (Striking Iron Gate guard).

Top-right:
This is the posta de donna (woman's guard) which for the full hand is the most beautiful. And of all sides she has great advantage. And the student watches the good work. And the right point is long and the left is short. And the fendente of the sword point is the striker. And it begins the forehand and above the flank, striking.

Label: Posta de donna destra e pulsativa (Right woman's guard, striking).

Bottom-left:
This is the posta de fenestra (window guard) which is the window that the window's fist fails. And it threatens to make all the guards. And do not stand broadly at the short guards. And whoever does not guard what the window is and the Hope. And the effort of that good one who changes and covers the guards and strikes and crosses and that goes for his guard.

Label: Posta de fenestra instabile (Unstable window guard).

Bottom-right:
This is the posta de donna la fenestra (woman's window guard) which is the woman and the Rose and is a brazen guard. And it calls and is certain of the windows and other openings. The straight lances and the two points voice companions. And above two guards of his guard.

Label: Posta de donna la fenestra pulsativa (Woman's window guard, striking).

## Notes
- Four figures showing sword guards (poste): posta de ferro pulsativa, posta de donna destra pulsativa, posta de fenestra instabile, and posta de donna la fenestra pulsativa.
- Each figure is crowned (indicating master status) and holds a longsword in different guard positions.
- This begins the section on two-handed sword guards (guardie de spade a doy mane).
- Text is in red and blue ink for decorated initials.

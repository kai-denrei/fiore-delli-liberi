## Notes
- Four figures arranged in two registers, each showing paired sword combat
- Upper pair shows two figures with raised swords in high guards
- Lower pair shows two figures in extended/long guards with swords pointed forward
- The crowned master figure appears on the left in both pairs
- Page number "41" visible in upper right corner (original foliation)
- Text describes guards (guardia) and techniques related to Fiore's system
- References to "posta de fierro" (iron gate guard) and "longa reguardia" (long guard)

# Folio 42v

## Transcription

Top:
¶ Questo fio contrario del Zugho che breuigo / che questo contrar che la lanza . la ponta bassa . p fora lo caualle . o in la testa . o in lo petto . ne la / compagnia chi po rebatter chi la stade anda basso .

Bottom:
¶ Io nesso e un altro contrario del lanza contra / spada . che questo tola lanza . uoltare e metter sua lanza / sotto la ponta contra . p che chi po rebattere . / ma lanza . ¶ E fon molto ponto fiore chi piu lanza . quella della spada .

## Translation

Top:
This is the counter to the Play that I describe, that this counter with the lance, the point low, through the horse, or in the head, or in the chest. In the company he who can beat aside, who stands low.

Bottom:
I am another counter of the lance against the sword, that this one takes the lance, turns and puts his lance under the opposing point, so that he who can beat aside, but the lance. And it was a very fine point, Fiore, he who [has] more lance, that of the sword.

## Notes
- Four mounted figures: two in top register, two in bottom register.
- Top-left figure wears a crown (master) and rides a rearing horse with lance; top-right holds a lance.
- Bottom pair shows two armored riders on horseback with lances engaging at close range.
- This page continues the discussion of lance vs. sword counters on horseback.
- The text is relatively brief compared to some other pages.

# Folio 41v

## Transcription

Top:
¶ Questa e la terza guardia de Lanza . dita Lanza . Questa maestra a contra Lanza . e ala guerra . E possa de donna la Plonfera . come seri vedesti . e vedureti ala via le compagnie .

Bottom:
¶ Anchora questa maestra porta le sie Lanze e posta de donna la Plonfera . E rebattere la lanza che la compira che inta Longijana . E possa rebatter e ella lanza fiorire e questo fin lanza . Lanze plonz / e quello fin tuto basterio a corno perche fio la ponta .

## Translation

Top:
This is the third guard of the lance, called Lance. This master [teaches] against the Lance and in war. And the guard of the lady the Plunging one, as you have seen and will see along the way with the companions.

Bottom:
Also this master carries her lances in the guard of the lady the Plunging one. And to beat aside the lance that will come from the long distance. And she can beat aside and with the lance flourish, and this ends the lance. Lances plunging, and that ends all that is sufficient at the horn because it is the point.

## Notes
- Four mounted figures: two in top register, two in bottom register.
- Top-right and bottom-right figures wear crowns (masters).
- The figures demonstrate lance guards and counters on horseback.
- Text is somewhat faded and difficult to read in places.
- No old foliation number visible on this verso page.

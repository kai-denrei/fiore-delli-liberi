# Folio 17r

## Transcription

Top-left:
¶ Lo Remedio d'Abrazare foy che poxo zugar de le braze /
mandritte e piu tosto che altro zogadore ay mia contra / e piu
de lor sono Magistro. ¶ Per lo ligadore de sotto /
che lo faro poy fara bonissimo ¶ Per la ligadore magistra /
de lo brazo che fizo morire grandeza de lo zogho /
¶ Nui semo tri posta zugadori per tal la voxe /
Primerana son poste possente de mio Fiorarme / de mia
la spada e la Daga in fodero. ¶ E questo ordina /
a noi lugano che po fare aldro: che faro d'arme /
guardara contra e targa.

Top-right:
¶ Questo e la contraria del Remedio de Abrazare che /
son Scolaro. ¶ E la partita chen faza al so [?] Scolaro che /
¶ Primerano diga che anche contrario fio de voy /
ognun torpe Brazzo de Dosso. e Segura e Falda Terreno /
a possa meterlo. ¶ La prima, al reduto le cose /
in la Bestia col oste [?] Scolaro.

Bottom-left:
¶ De ogne ognialta foy a rimedio foy una buona /
presa. ¶ E lo Scolaro che cum lo Magistro che testa /
anda foy paia al braza destretore dichaco la praticha /
e chi parte de una destreza per poi morirte [?] como per che /
per si lo batagliero in la man e la persona de foy buona /
che lo possa presto alzar de la forma morira e contra /
della foy de foy. ¶ Altramente posso zugatello [?] la foy /
morire in la contraria de la mane [?] sotto la foy faza /
morire la porta de sua foy posta. ¶ De tal foy pezarma /
Donita le la la mani fermi: ancora ogniquella foy e /
foy de botta.
la solomazo [?] zoge che Scoldo abbandono la la mane buona.

Bottom-right:
¶ Noi foy lo contrario del remedio longo ohe mia destreza /
¶ de foy e che sian le le mane posso contra /
che al foy scolpare. ¶ Primetrano e contra de io che /
sone Brace e forza: e scolpdo de la /

## Translation

Top-left:
The Remedy of Wrestling was that I can play with the right arms, and more quickly than any other player against me, and I am Master of them. Through the lower bind that I will make, it will be very good. Through the master bind of the arm that makes the greatness of the play die. We are three guard players by such voice. The first are powerful guards of my Flower of Arms: of my sword and the Dagger in its sheath. And this orders us to play what can do other: who will make arms, will guard against the shield.

Top-right:
This is the counter of the Remedy of Wrestling, for I am the Scholar. And the departure that the Scholar makes. The first says that also I am a counter to you, each one takes the arm from the back. And Segura and Falda Terreno can put him down. The first, to reduce things into the Beast with the Scholar.

Bottom-left:
Of every highest play was a remedy, was a good grip. And the Scholar who with the Master seizes, went to the right arm destroyer, demonstrating the practice, and who departs with a skill to then kill, as for the combatant in the hand and the person of the play, who can quickly raise from the form will die and counter of the play. Otherwise I can play the counter of the hand under the play, to kill the gate of his guard.

Bottom-right:
We are the counter of the long remedy, oh my skill, of the play and that the hands I can counter, who at the play escapes. The first and counter of I who am Arms and force: and escaping from that which other moves: that the arm and with the Mouth.

## Notes
- Four pairs of wrestling (abrazare) figures, each pair showing two combatants grappling
- All figures are unarmored, wearing only hose/tights
- The crowned figure appears to be the Master (Magistro/Remedio) in the top-left pair
- Some text is difficult to read due to fading and scribal abbreviations; several uncertain readings marked with [?]
- Page numbered "19" in upper right corner (old foliation) and "12" at bottom

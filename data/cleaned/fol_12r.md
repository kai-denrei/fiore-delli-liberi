## Translation

Top-left:
I am the counter and in this way I stand so that you cannot put the long [guard] nor bind me with the long [guard]. And there is no sluggish hand that will remain nor tear me apart, and to put you down [I] want to falsify what you strike at me, and it is bad and marvelous that the blow is covered.

Top-right:
This cover is called "more Florence" [stronger flourish], and that is, the blow and the dust so [?] passed the play to flourish in advance, and so it is worth nothing to me as a cover, and also you, long boy, [come] after to instruct.

Bottom-left:
This is the counter of this play that is no less than dishonorable by flourish. And the cover goes to the soft cover of Florence [flourish]. All that the cover and to flourish -- it does not restrain me.

Bottom-right:
Two good grips that [throw] to the ground. One [is] my blow, but you break the long [guard], fixed the thing -- marvelous blow. And so his long [guard] behind to flourish, and this thing is now the girl/manner of striking.

## Notes
Four pairs of wrestlers. Top-left pair shows one figure wearing a crown (master) defending against a grip. Top-right shows two figures exchanging strikes/grips. Bottom-left shows a crowned figure (master) in a grappling exchange. Bottom-right shows a close grappling throw with a dagger visible on the ground between the figures. Text contains many uncertain readings due to script difficulty.

# Folio 37r

## Transcription

Top-left:
¶ Per mia rason questo chi ve dapri in rasgue de una dago
forte con la roscha e che me fuo frangheza niunamente
in la feritate lo braço la ferma che gie voi de abri-
sello lo scolaro.

Top-right:
¶ A me mossa una idea in manire questo daga de lo
maiestro. E dolore de vostro molto a grande e grosa
quadermente e yo le braço in la man rosta come gio
prouare che la mia de brode tu ragione nostro arme in la
coda.

Bottom-left:
¶ De maistro zingho e della forlana che me domandare che ben dagho
lo quelle dello rebatere che en dale conguere colare. Siamo
lo la botta vole altre ben possa fanno. E quello
que ello se re la viafarcha che la
questa ve fosse po se me punta.

Bottom-right:
¶ Quello che dise lo forlano che domandare in quelle
sara fora segue che se la ragione a [?] la contrario Guadache
le[?] lo rebatire questa te forire che le abragione e che me
forire che alle destre e questo de bey fanare.

## Translation

Top-left:
By my reason, this one who opens before you in the drawing of a strong dagger with the hilt, and my frankness in no way in the ferocity, the arm holds firm, for you wish to open the scholar.

Top-right:
An idea came to me in the manner of this dagger of the master. And the pain of your much great and heavy fourfold, and I the arm in the hand turns as I prove that my way of broth, you reason our arms in the tail.

Bottom-left:
The master Zingho and the Forlana who ask me, for I give well the beating back that in the conquering flows. Let us the blow willingly, other well they can make. And that which is the wayfaring, for this, you were able for my point.

Bottom-right:
That which the Forlano says who asks, in those he will follow outside, for the reason to [?] the counter. Gain the beating back, this strikes you, for the embrace, and that strikes me at the right, and this well to make.

## Notes
- Four pairs of figures in two registers depicting sword combat
- Upper figures show close-range engagement with swords
- Lower figures show more extended combat with longer measure
- The crowned figure (master) appears in upper positions
- Page number "39" visible in upper right corner (original foliation)
- Text references "maistro zingho" and "forlana/forlano" which may be play names or figure descriptions
- Several words are difficult to decipher due to the handwriting style

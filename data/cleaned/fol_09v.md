## Notes
This page has an unusual layout. Top section has two text blocks flanking illustrations (top-left: two figures grappling/fighting with daggers; top-right: text only with a small partial figure). Middle section has a single standing figure holding a dagger in each hand with arms spread wide, wearing a crown — this appears to be a master figure demonstrating a guard position. A large block of text spans the bottom of the page. This appears to be a summary or rubric text for the dagger section, as the text itself mentions "la rubrica" (the rubric). The crowned central figure with two daggers is distinctive. Text is very challenging throughout — heavily abbreviated, faded, and in dense Venetian dialect.

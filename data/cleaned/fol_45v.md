## Notes
- Four mounted combat pairs arranged in quadrants.
- All figures are engaged in close mounted combat with swords.
- Significant overlap in language with fol_45r; these two pages form a continuous sequence of plays and counters.
- Text continues to be extremely difficult to read; script is small and faded in many places.
- References to "Zugho" (play), "Rimedio" (remedy), and "contrario" (counter) structure the techniques.

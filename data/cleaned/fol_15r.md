## Notes
Four pairs of wrestlers. Top-left shows two figures in close grappling with one applying what appears to be a headlock or neck control. Top-right shows two figures standing apart in guard positions. Bottom-left shows two figures in an intricate grappling exchange with longer text (most text on the page). Bottom-right shows two figures wrestling with one applying a hold. This page has the densest text of the set so far. The script is extremely difficult and many readings are speculative. The text block at bottom-left is notably longer than most captions on other pages.

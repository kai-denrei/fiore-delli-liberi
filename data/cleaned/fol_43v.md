# Folio 43v

## Transcription

Top:
¶ Anchora altro portare de Spada fio e dissipara possa de cola / lanzaro . e fio motto done contra lanza . e contra sercola [?] / e contracauallo di suo per e spada / rolla star trinciare . ¶ E come hoy a menar ello la pena . a la testa . manda felo sesso . ¶ E lo colpo de ferendo [?] e ricamasso . ella se plu da fontario contra / parte la spada dello fior contrigo . e poi fiore chi corgo / frontudo lo figamo lo eparsse .

Bottom:
¶ Anchora questa spada . fone quadra . de estaba leuare / fio lanza . e gialdo hoy che foni incumanar . ella spada . de / lanza manda como a moi presta contra armezada . E posta . / dou questo e qui contra . e contra rotta chi po de porere . / dorese . a la por manda . ¶ E per notte questi / che la rebia . in contra Spada dello fior leuare posse . / la colla lanza chi po e diuoto de colla lanza .

## Translation

Top:
Also another carry of the Sword done and separated, guard of the lancer, and done with many women [techniques] against lance and against circling, and counter-horse from his power and sword, the standing cut shall be. And as today to lead with the pain to the head, send the blow itself. And the striking blow and recovered, and if more from afar against, parts the sword of the flower against. And then Fiore who runs, a frontal we figure the parting.

Bottom:
Also this sword, it was square, from the stable rising the lance, and the shield today that was beginning, and the sword, from lance sends as to me ready against armored. And guard, where this and here counter, and counter broken he who can from power, backs to the sending. And for the night those who have rage, in counter Sword of the flower rising I can, the with the lance he who can and devoted with the lance.

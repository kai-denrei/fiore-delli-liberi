## Translation

Top-left:
Long guard, called the first unstable. And it is a guard much for doing and for doing what the mistress of the guard [teaches] and for putting and lifting the sword. And to make the point, quickly the thrust and then finish, all the great guards. And taken the Frontal and the first above. And the head and the first rise of the head demand. The striking above.

Label: Posta longa instabile (Long guard, unstable).

Top-right:
This is the middle defensive guard and there are five. Which are strong and four, and she who makes a guard makes a right carry and backhand, and the stable in the rise, armed and the foundation and the turn, and all. And she who stands at the point of master Fiore, great one who does the harm of the sword's movement, and the turn. And that of thrusts forward, all forward of Fiore.

Label: Forma di ferro mezana stabile (Middle iron form, stable).

Bottom-left:
This is the long guard that is seen from afar. Guard and master and the fendente and the striking. Master to finish can sing of the fist, and cannot the company. From the guard is great of the guard and the case of the conquest.

Label: Posta breve stabile (Short guard, stable).

Bottom-right:
This is the woman of the companion for her of the great long guards the knot of the plot. And with great failure and failure and of the tin and of the swift master. And she who is near the turn that to that good one of the flanks comes close to the foot of the left guard. Guard and the woman of the Frontal of the flank the point of the rise of the guards. Of the one found and so she the part of the work. And the other of the gates. Of the one the foot that is the defense.

Label: Donna di congiunture stabile (Woman of the joints, stable).

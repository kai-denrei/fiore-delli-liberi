## Notes
- Page numbered "21" in upper right corner (old foliation) and "21" at bottom
- This page marks the transition from wrestling (abrazare) to sword and dagger (spada e daga) combat
- Four pairs of figures, now armed with swords and daggers
- Top figures appear in guard positions; bottom figures show engaged combat
- The crowned Master figure is visible in the top-left pair
- Text references "Spada e daga" (sword and dagger), "Remedio" (remedy/master), and "Scolaro" (scholar)

# Folio 44r

## Transcription

Top-left:
¶ Questo e la prima Zugho che eisse de la guardia / piciola lanza che qui monstro . che stabille molto ro / tara la Spada . Delle sue riuoltare . e metterse cha la posta / in lo ponto . ouero in la sosta como qui dipartito .

Top-right:
¶ Questo fio la segonda Zugho che per do / quatro reponere . le Remi contrari dopano la testa / che vogi a lor delli chi e mortale ala testa .

Bottom-left:
¶ Questo e un altro Zugho lo terço . che rebattudo / ella Spada della fior dissipare . alla propria . e la manere / ponto che filo alla testa la Spada . e messi chi possi fiorir / lo ponto .

Bottom-right:
¶ Questo fio la quarto Zugho che la Podeno / chi sol ferir . la taglia . a trar che la Spada . p fillo / modo che caderno que impartito .

## Translation

Top-left:
This is the first Play that comes from the guard of the small lance that I show here, that stable greatly will turn the Sword. Of its turnings, and to put oneself in the position at the point, or in the pause as here departed.

Top-right:
This is the second Play that for the four repositions, the Remedy-counters cover the head, that they wish to strike them who is mortal to the head.

Bottom-left:
This is another Play, the third, that having beaten aside the Sword of the flower to separate, at the proper one, and the manner point that strikes at the head the Sword, and put who can flourish the point.

Bottom-right:
This is the fourth Play that the Power who alone will strike, the cut, to draw the Sword, in such a manner that they fall here departed.

## Notes
- Four mounted combat pairs, one in each quadrant of the page.
- Page numbered "46" in upper right corner (old foliation).
- Each pair demonstrates a numbered play (zugho): first through fourth.
- All figures are mounted and engaged in sword combat on horseback.
- The plays appear to be sequential techniques from a single guard (guardia piciola lanza).
- Top-left and bottom-left text blocks have red initial capitals; top-right and bottom-right have blue/dark initials.

# Folio 27v

## Transcription

Top-left:
¶ Che nostro zogo Rebatuda, punta falza e punta curva. ¶ E lo dire como le faze, lo ma[?] esto in le volte restare e cossa ella pe volta non mandar[e]. ¶ E lo falo[?] ella la contremessa, e sopra delle foy Coscha le foy ponta morte. ¶ E lo faro detto la spada mia si ella la mano dreta. El primo de movir[e] Frontale la golla e la ella la gamba, lo magistro questo zogo in arme che forza.

Top-right:
¶ Mastro ella la contremesa del coszo che me donanty de ponta falza contra de pontta curva. ¶ E lo per la mia possta, ella la cabeza drit[t]a e la ella per le foy grossa. ¶ E lo destro ella la mia contremeza e la ponta grossa le la contremeza. ¶ De lo Rebatitura. ¶ E lo per possta e io ponto la contraria la e lo che a e piaza. ¶ E lo volter. ¶ Ella questa contremeza l'arme e la piaza.

Bottom-left:
¶ Denanti finesse zogo longo della spada, a doy mane, che per sova zogo vero chi mane con zogo le per veza contremessa, de per ve breve, e lo per volta, e di turmenta e divercament[e], e di tutta che se queste contremess[e] fovero e lo quandra e che tute le grossa[?] frontale menbar[e] el per ponto.

Bottom-right:
¶ Denanti comenza zogo de spade a doy mane, gostilisemo e bello e de foy lo per spada. ¶ E lo fiore vore e lo se breve de posta. ¶ E lo per la pontta di modo e contremeza de per lo forza. ¶ E la perforante raptore[?] ella le frontale a offendere e la deffendere, ello la compania ella lo Rebatimento ella le forza contra e lo frontale contra, la de lo contremeza la cossa de per lo posta contremeza ella spada.

## Translation

Top-left:
That our play the rebatting, false thrust and curved thrust. And to say how to make the face, this in the turns to remain and thing and the foot turn not to send. And the feint and the counter-measure, and above the thigh was the death point. And I shall make said my sword and the right hand. The first to move the Frontal the throat and the leg, the master this play in arms that is force.

Top-right:
Master and the counter-measure of the blow that you give me of false thrust against curved thrust. And through my guard, the right head and through the thick. And the right and my counter-measure and the thick point the counter-measure. Of the Rebatting. And through the guard and I point the contrary and that which pleases. And the turning. And this counter-measure the arms and the plaza.

Bottom-left:
Before this finishes the long play of the sword, with two hands, that through his true play who the hand with play sees the counter-measure, through the short, and through the turn, and of torment and diversely, and of all that these counter-measures were and the square and that all the thick frontal remember the point.

Bottom-right:
Before this begins the play of the two-handed sword, most just and beautiful and of the sword. And the flower wishes and the short of the guard. And through the thrust in manner and counter-measure through force. And the perforating raptor and the frontal to offend and to defend, and the company and the rebatting and the force against and the frontal against, of the counter-measure the thing of the guard counter-measure and sword.

## Notes
- Four combat pairs showing two-handed sword plays.
- Top section deals with "rebatuda" (rebatting/parrying) techniques including punta falza (false thrust) and punta curva (curved thrust).
- Bottom-left text explicitly mentions the end of the "zogo longo della spada" (long play of the sword).
- Bottom-right text introduces a new play section described as "gostilisemo e bello" (most just and beautiful).
- The transition between long sword play and a new section is notable on this page.

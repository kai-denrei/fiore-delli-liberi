## Notes
Four wrestling scenes in a 2x2 grid, page numbered "10" in upper right. Top-left: Two wrestlers in close grapple, one (larger figure) seizing the other at the chest/torso area. Top-right: A crowned figure (master) in a defensive posture against an approaching opponent. Bottom-left: Two wrestlers, one controlling the other's arm in what appears to be an arm lock. Bottom-right: Two figures in a striking/grappling exchange at distance — this may be transitioning toward dagger combat. Text is quite faded and the handwriting is challenging in many places; several readings are uncertain.

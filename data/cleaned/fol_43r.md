# Folio 43r

## Transcription

Top:
¶ Io nesso ella la Spada . Guardia questa alla la logi / ella guerra alo leuare de caualluçaro . Cum in questo della lanza . e lo cauallo apresso . lo maistro de la Spada / insegna fira lanza in fiorir . in contra quatro . Remedie . el fierir se per lo malstre sono la Spada e sallo questo / a menar e ferine de ben arlene de Spada .

Bottom:
¶ Io nesso e lo contrario della taglia de lanza . e dou / Spado che dissepare . que che questo dia la lanza . e fora . lo contra la rimedio della fior e breuigo . Se questo della Spada . e che chi po dissipare la lanza cum la / spada si bassa .

## Translation

Top:
I am the Sword. This guard at the place of war at the rising of the horseman. With this of the lance and the horse near, the master of the Sword teaches to strike the lance in the flourish, in counter four Remedies. And the striking through the master are the Sword and knows this, to lead and strike from the good art of the Sword.

Bottom:
I am the counter of the cut of the lance, and where the Sword separates, that this one gives the lance and outside. The counter the remedy of the flower and I describe. If this of the Sword, and he who can separate the lance with the sword so low.

## Notes
- Four mounted figures: two in top register, two in bottom register.
- Page numbered "45" in upper right corner (old foliation).
- Top-left figure is armored with sword; top-right wears a crown (master) and carries weapons.
- Bottom pair shows a mounted lance thrust being countered; the left figure has struck with a lance low, the right figure sits mounted.
- This section deals with sword vs. lance techniques on horseback.
- Text is faded and partially illegible in several places.

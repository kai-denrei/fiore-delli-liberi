## Notes
Four scenes in a 2x2 grid. This page marks a transition — the top two scenes show unarmed wrestling (figures are unclothed/in hose), while the bottom two scenes show figures wearing blue clothing and holding daggers (short blades visible). Top-left: Two wrestlers, one attempting the "luchertola" (lizard) grip. Top-right: Two wrestlers in a standing grapple, one wearing a crown. Bottom-left: Two figures in blue garments, one seated on a stool/bench while the other stands with a dagger — this appears to be a dagger defense scenario. Bottom-right: Two figures in blue with daggers engaged in combat. This folio represents the transition from the abrazare (wrestling) section to the daga (dagger) section. Text is heavily faded and very difficult to read in places.
